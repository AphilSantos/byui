Overview

Purpose

The aim of this site is to be a simple project portfolio with the sources open for anyone to view.
It is the hope of the author to spread a free source of learning to anyone interested in becoming a
web developer enthusiast.

Audience

It is the goal of this site to be readable to every audience at any age.
It will avoid typical jargons and will keep the language simple and clear. Colors will match
the theme of the site which gives the futuristic tech vibe.

Content

Home page

Purpose

Helping visitors be informed, expand their options, and make an informed decision. Both the homepage and contact page
will be as simple as answering "what they will get from my services", "why they should avail for my help", and "how
they will be able to avail." More information about my services will be appropriated in my portfolio page. By the end
of this page alone, people should already be intrested to know more about my offers.

What you will find

In this page of the site visitors will find details about myself and the things that I specialize.
My scope is front-end development that encompass the design structure of any website.
They will also find some of my work experiences and timeline. Educational background will also be
included for additional credibility.

  • About Me section

    This section will contain a picture of myself and few details about my upbringing.
    My skills and expertise will be made mention as well. Other links such as from
    linkedIn, Facebook, and Github will be listed to invite visitor to know more about me.

Paid Offers

I offer front-end development services. From building from scratch to redesigning. Website multiple platform
responsiveness will, as usual, be part of the service as package or as and entirely separate service altogether.
Other inclusions such us rushed projects will also available.

Free Offers

In gratitude for the free education I received from my seniors and other respected tech organizations; I offer
free to view code and free to copy-and-paste ideas from this site.
Viewers will be informed here in the homepage about this feature. I hope to be a resource for aspiring coders like
I have been. They can integrate the codes as shown in the sites including the imported such as stylesheets and so on
without any need for permission or any of the like.

[Portfolio]

What you will find

This will showcase most of the finished projects as well as a view of the code integrated when the
showcased sections are clicked. Visitors will also find testimonials from clients that had once availed for
professional services.
This part of the site will also inform them of the successes previous clients have attained from their built stores.

Purpose

The goal is to help the visitors become future clients. When they find that I am capable of the task
they need accomplished, they will then be invited to book a call and avail for my services. Another purpose
will be to allow other developers to learn from my finished projects through the free-to-view sources from
this site.

Secondary purpose

This page of the site will also serve as an additional credibility when I intend to apply for an occupational
opportunity from a corporation. Like most resumes and vitae now gives more encouragements to professional looking
portfolios and that websites are now accepted as such. Having a portfolio-site like this will be a additional for my
credibility.

[Contact Us]

What you will find

Like other typical advertising and portfolio sites, visitors will find that they will be able to contact me
via email, phone call, or through calendar booking. Pricing will not be shown as projects and expenses will be
discussed during the call/appointment.

What will they need to do

They will be required to provide their working email address. They also be asked to answer certain questions such as
What service/s will they want to avail. Other information will also include their contact preference whether be it through
phone call via WhatsApp, video conference calls through Zoom, or simply chatting through google hangouts. The goal is to offer
flexibility and accomodate the needs and convenience of the clients.
